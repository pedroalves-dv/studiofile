How the sticky works

The <section> is min-h-dvh on mobile and h-[300vh] on md+. The TOTEM wrapper inside it is sticky top-0 h-dvh flex items-center justify-center — so it occupies one full viewport height and sticks at the top, giving perfect vertical centering for the duration of the scroll. The image columns sit behind it as an absolute inset-0 layer.
